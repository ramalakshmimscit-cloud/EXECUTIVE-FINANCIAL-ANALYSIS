# EXECUTIVE FINANCIAL ANALYSIS 

## 📊 Project Overview
This Power BI project provides a comprehensive financial analysis of organizational performance, tracking key metrics across global regions, market segments, and product lines for the 2013-2014 fiscal period.

## 🔍 Data Visualizations & Insights

### 1. Profit by Segments (Clustered Bar Chart)
*   **Government:** $11.39M (Highest Profit)
*   **Small Business:** $4.14M
*   **Channel Partners:** $1.32M
*   **Midmarket:** $0.85M
*   **Enterprise:** ($0.61M) (Net Loss)

### 2. Max Units Sold by Day Type (Stacked Bar Chart)
*   **Dimensions:** Weekday vs. Weekend.
*   **Legend:** Discount Bands (High, Medium, None).
*   **Insight:** Sales volume is significantly higher on weekdays, with "High" discount bands driving a large portion of the volume.

### 3. Sales by Year (Donut Chart)
*   **Comparison:** 2013 vs. 2014.
*   **Insight:** 2014 represents the majority of the total sales revenue compared to 2013.

### 4. Total Cost by Country (Map)
*   **Geographies:** North America, South America, and Europe.
*   **Scale:** Country-specific costs range from $1.6M to $2.1M.
